Our History

Bharatiya Samata Sanjeevani Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Rawanakola is a social organization founded in 1989 by Hon. Shri Sangramji Waghmare. Since its inception, the organization has undertaken various initiatives for the welfare of society. Recognizing the lack of educational facilities for rural and underprivileged children, Mahatma Gandhi Vidyalaya was established in 1991. Initially, due to financial constraints, the school operated in temporary makeshift arrangements. Later, a small plot was acquired, and the school started functioning in tin-roofed classrooms.

Currently, around 150 to 180 students are enrolled in the school. However, due to inadequate infrastructure and temporary structures, students face numerous challenges. During heavy rainfall, water seeps into the classrooms. Lightning and thunder cause fear among the students. During the summer, extreme heat makes it difficult to study, leading to frequent school closures. Considering these issues, we propose the construction of a permanent school building.

CSR-Backed Initiatives

Enhanced Learning Experience: Improved infrastructure will create a better environment for education. Increased Attendance: Safe and modern classrooms will encourage parents to send their children to school regularly. Improved Educational Quality: Access to digital classrooms and laboratories will provide students with modern learning opportunities.

Current Infrastructure Challenges

The classrooms are made of tin sheets, causing extreme heat in summer, making learning difficult. Roof leakages during monsoons lead to water accumulation inside classrooms, often forcing school closures. Lack of proper ventilation and lighting affects students' ability to concentrate and study. No digital learning equipment is available. There is inadequate seating furniture for students. No science laboratory or library facilities.

Proposed Project – Construction of a New School Building

We plan to construct a safe and modern school building with the following features: 14 to 15 well-equipped classrooms (each 18x20 feet) Digital classroom Well-stocked library Science laboratory Proper ventilation and natural lighting Comfortable and modern furniture for students

Estimated Cost

The total estimated cost of the project is approximately ₹60 to ₹70 lakh. This includes the complete construction of the school building along with necessary facilities. Currently, local MLA Sanjay Bansode has sanctioned ₹10 lakh from the MLA fund, and construction of one classroom has commenced. However, additional funding is required to complete the entire project.

Organization’s Responsibility and Eligibility

The organization possesses valid 80G certificate, PAN card, an official bank account, and other necessary documentation. The management is fully committed to providing quality education. School Clerk, Mr. Krishnaraj Sangramji Waghmare, will serve as the official representative for the CSR proposal.
