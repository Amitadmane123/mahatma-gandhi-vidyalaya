Our Admission Process

Step

01

Inquiry & Application

Visit our school or website to collect the admission form. Get details about eligibility, fees, and available seats.

Step

02

Document Submission

Submit the filled admission form along with required documents such as birth certificate, Aadhaar card, and photographs.

Step

03

Entrance Test

Depending on the grade level, students may be required to take an entrance test to assess their academic proficiency.

Step

04

Parent & Student Interview

A short interaction session with the principal or admission team to discuss expectations and school policies.

Step

05

Admission Confirmation

Once selected, parents will receive an admission confirmation letter along with details of fee payment and joining formalities.

Required Documents

Birth Certificate

A copy of the student's birth certificate is required for age verification.

Aadhaar Card

A valid Aadhaar card of the student or guardian for identification.

Passport-size Photographs

Two recent passport-size photographs of the student.

Previous School Report Card

Required if the student is transferring from another school.
